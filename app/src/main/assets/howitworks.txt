Payday loans have a simple application process. You provide your identification, banking, and other details, and once approved, receive your loan funds either right away or within 24 hours.
The business explains its service as offering a much-needed option to people who can use a little help from time to time. The company makes money through upfront loan fees and interest charges on existing loans.
Payday loan companies operate under a wide variety of titles, and payday loans usually run less than $500.00. Payday lenders may take postdated checks as collateral, and generally, they charge a significant fee for their loans which equates to a very high interest rate, with annualized rates as high as four hundred percent.
Many states have been working on passing regulations to place caps on the allowable interest rates and help out consumers that rely on these loans.
